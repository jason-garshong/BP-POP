About Us

BigPay is a global payments network that offers Payments as a Service to Peer-to-Peer business owners in Ghana with customers worldwide. Backed by the latest state-of-the-art technology, BigPay makes cross-border payments flow securely, facilitating same-day payouts to your local bank or eWallet conveniently.

We leverage the diversity of blue-chip financial institutions ranging from Banks and Telecommunication providers to Money Transfer Operators and Payment Service Providers to create an ecosystem that allows businesses to unleash their full potential. Your growth is at the nexus of all our daily endeavors.
